Who am I?

As I'm always used to say, my occupation found me and I wasn't aiming at becoming a digital professional. While I studied for my M.A. in Communications at the Hebrew University, I joined a student-based NGO where I instantly became a team leader creating its new website.

Leading other people meant I always had to be one step ahead so I've decided to go through training in Social Media managment and was promoted to lead an even larger team of students.

I can truley say that my most important lesson in life is that initiative matters. This lesson kept helping me realize many more goals further in my life. Advicing to some of the largest brands in the Israeli market in the past few years, I'm always happy to help new clients create their own unique voice and tone and reach their goals!
